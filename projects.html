YOUR Roguelike Adventure

A short roguelike shooter with dynamic difficulty. I worked with 2 other programmers to develop the game's functionalities. I've worked on the random level generation, map selection, enemy AI, and various other core mechanics. Working with other programmers allowed me to I learned many new tools and tricks in Unity. The experience of working under a lead designer was also interesting, though sometimes frustrating.

Salvation

A 2D Metroidvania I developed over the summer for XPrize Connect's Code Games. This was my first time using Unity and I got overambitious with the project. I was limit testing myself; every subsequent boss introducing at least one new mechanic (attack indicators, player tracking, bullet hell, summons, etc.). Though it didn't make it to the final round, this project was a great learning experience.
